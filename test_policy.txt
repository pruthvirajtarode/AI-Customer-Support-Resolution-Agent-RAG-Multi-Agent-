SupportAI Refund Policy
-----------------------
1. Standard items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
2. Perishable items, such as food and flowers, are strictly non-refundable and non-returnable.
3. Final sale items cannot be returned or refunded.
4. Hygiene products (masks, undergarments) cannot be returned for safety reasons.
5. Opened software or electronic media cannot be returned.
6. Orders can be cancelled at any time before they have been shipped. Once shipped, they must follow the return policy.
